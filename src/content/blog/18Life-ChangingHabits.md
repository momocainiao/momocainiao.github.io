### 14. Maintain Key Networks
*   **The Insight:** 85% of high-paying jobs are found through **"Weak Ties"** (acquaintances).

*   **The Mindset:** When you feel low, just hit the baseline. This prevents the "all-or-nothing" cycle and keeps momentum alive.
